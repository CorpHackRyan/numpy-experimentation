What does your resting heart rate say about you?

And what does your present resting heart rate say about your future self? High blood pressure, stress, anxiety, lack of exercise and being overweight - are just a few of the things that can increase your resting heart rate. And according to the study above, a resting heart rate higher than 75 beats per minute (bpm) can increase your risk of early death by a combined 57%! Your resting heart rate is one way to determine your overall physical health. And improving your physical health, diet and exercise scientifically will improve your chances at longevity in this life.
